Subject Search

Type a Library of Congress subject heading, then press the Enter key or click Go.

If you are unsure of the formal subject heading, try a Keyword Search.

Search Tips

Type as much or as little of the subject as you want. For example:

  • women's rights
  • Austen, Jane, 1775-1817--Biography
  • United States History Civil War, 1861-1865

The Library of Congress subject search uses a specific, controlled vocabulary for describing Library material. If you do not find what you are looking for in the subject index, try a keyword search or ask a librarian for assistance.
