Request Verification
  • Entering a date here is OPTIONAL. Entering a date will not help you get the book faster. If you don't have a deadline just click "GO".
  • If you prefer to enter a date, allow at least two weeks if you are placing a recall on a book that is checked out. Allow at least FIVE days if you are placing a request for retrieval from storage. If you choose an earlier date, your request may be cancelled before the item is ready to pick up.
  • If you are requesting a book that is ON ORDER, do not enter a date here. It is not possible to expedite outstanding orders and the Libraries can't control when the item will arrive.
  • Click "GO" to proceed to the item selection screen.
